    At FarmAid we believe that with millions of rural internet users coming online for the first time on mobile, it is time they get the benefits. We wish to build a transparent supply system such that the farmers can sell their produce directly to sellers all over India.

    Read more
    Our Mission

    FarmAid will create a platform for transparent pricing of tea based on its quality which is completely online and this will help the farmers make the efficient use of smartphone technology which has reached to every corner of the countrys.

    Our Vision

    We aim to remove the middile man system and bring the farmers their deserved profits. Buyers will be then approached by the company with the benefit of competitive pricing, quality focus and consistent availability.

    Will this provide any benefits?

    • No wastage of farmer’s produce
    • Better prices to the farmers for their produce
    • Consistent and enhanced quality of product in the market
    • Lower rates of the products in the market as a result of the better supply chain and competitive pricing
