Our Service

We provide reasonable prices to the farmers and quality assured products to the buyers. They now have the advantage to select the best quality produce and rely on FarmAid’s consistent availability. 100% traceability on crop’s origin and route will also be provided to bring about further trust and transparency in this process.

Sourcing

In this step, products harvested by the farmers will be sources from the farmers. Ground-level employees will be responsible for this work and will make sure that timely sourcing is done

Sorting

After the sourcing has been done, it is very important to sort the products in the warehouse. Sorting has various benefits ranging from taking less time to packaging to efficient management and utilisation of resources.

Grading

After the sorting of the products is done, it needs to be graded in the order of the quality and then put up on the website for the buyers to choose and purchase from as promised by the startup.

Packaging

Based on the orders received from the buyers, proper packaging of the products will be done such that the buyers do not receive degraded products.

Dispatch

The packages will be delivered by the startup as promised by the startup. Ordered packages will be dispatched.

Delivery

Delivery of the products will be divided into the following two categories: intercity transport and intracity transport.
